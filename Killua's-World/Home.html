Welcome to My World!

I have short, soft grey hair that compliments my piercing green eyes, which always seem to capture attention. I take my meals very seriously, and I expect nothing but the best. Overall, I am a curious and adventurous feline who enjoys exploring new places and meeting new people.

What I like to Do

Speaking of play, I love to play fetch - yes, you heard that right, cats can fetch toys too!
